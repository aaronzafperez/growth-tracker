Privacy Policy

Time Investment Tracker — Effective May 7, 2026

Overview

Time Investment Tracker is a personal time-tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

Data Collection

Time Investment Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

Data Storage

All data you create in the app — including tasks, sessions, and goals — is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any server or cloud service. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or servers. There are no accounts, logins, or external connections of any kind.

Widgets and Live Activities

The app offers home screen widgets and Live Activities (Dynamic Island). These features access your locally stored data through on-device shared storage (App Groups) and do not transmit any information externally.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
